What Stood Out

The main signals visible at that point

The presence of a control plane becomes a platform differentiator

Across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and Microsoft, sessions, policy, traces, and approvals have become shared points of comparison. The benchmark for evaluating platforms has expanded clearly.

Protocols and interoperability reshape the lock-in discussion

A2A and MCP-related work suggest that vendor changes and new integrations can be absorbed at the architecture layer. Lock-in is no longer just a model problem.

The practical path favors an accumulation of bounded workflows

Looking across 2025, the more practical path is not a single universal agent but a portfolio of bounded workflows with explicit ownership. That approach also tends to move faster in deployment.

Use Cases

Use cases that already looked practical at that time

A recurring research desk

  • Run recurring collection, comparison, and draft generation on a weekly or monthly basis.
  • With protocol and evaluation discipline in place, the quality of recurring runs is easier to stabilize.

Finance and operations exception handling

  • Use agents not for every standard process but for triaging and drafting around exceptions and edge cases.
  • This is where bounded workflows plus approval chains fit especially well.

Operating Implications

What had to be decided first in design, evaluation, and operations

Observed implication

As a year-opening synthesis, the key question is not how many pilots exist but whether the organization has reusable control planes and policy layers.

  • Before launching more proof-of-concepts, establish shared layers for session, identity, and approval.
  • Redefine responsibility boundaries across security, product, and operations around agent rollout.
  • Compare vendors not only on models but also on protocol and control-plane maturity.

Key Takeaway

The provisional conclusion that could already be drawn

Looking back from January 2026, the heart of agent adoption had already shifted from what the model can do to what can be delegated, supervised, and stopped cleanly.
